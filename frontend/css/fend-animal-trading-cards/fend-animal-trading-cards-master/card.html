Elephant

Elephants live a long time.

  • Family: Elephantidae
  • Order: Proboscidea
  • Adult Weight: 7000kg
  • Age: 70 years

Female elephants live in family groups with one dominant female assuming the role of a matriarch. Adult male elephants, on the other hand, live alone or with other male elephants and come into contact with family groups only during mating season. Elephants are intelligent and appear to be self aware.
